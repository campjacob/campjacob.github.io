## Unit Introduction and What You Will Learn

Week six is asynchronous. Students will read about evaluation in macro practice. There are forums for students to connect with content from the course, explore literature about program evaluation, and consider their community impact project and assessment. Some of the learning objectives this week include:

- Recognize the context of program evaluation.
- Identify problems and barriers in program evaluation.
- Examine real-world examples of data collection and evaluation processes.

## Unit Assignments

**Content**

- Read Kirst-Ashman and Hull (2018) _Chapter 10: Evaluation in Macro Practice_

**W-06 A-01: Asynchronous Participation and Engagement**

The expectation is that each of your replies will be substantive and provide meaningful perspectives, contributing to the forum's conversation and scholarship. They can be related to the prompts or building on conversations shared by peers. There are _six forums_ for this week, and you are expected to make at least **6 replies** across any of the forums and read all of your peers' replies. These forums include the following:

- [Community Impact Project Assessment Ideas](https://myheritage.heritage.edu/ICS/Academics/SOWK/SOWK_531/2526_SP-SOWK_531-0/💻_W-06_223-31.jnz?portlet=Group_Discussion_Forums&screen=PostView&screenType=change&id=53155102-a99a-4a48-adfc-8a67f38e88ed) invites students to share how they are thinking about assigning for  their community impact project.
- A hospital-based discharge planning scenario serves as the basis for [Program Evaluation Case Study](https://myheritage.heritage.edu/ICS/Academics/SOWK/SOWK_531/2526_SP-SOWK_531-0/💻_W-06_223-31.jnz?portlet=Group_Discussion_Forums&screen=PostView&screenType=change&id=0f51fb39-7888-4c00-a3c0-a6f8a5543a3f), where students work through selecting evaluation goals, designs, and data collection methods.
- Students respond to a range of textbook questions on program evaluation concepts and barriers in [Questions from Chapter 10 Content](https://myheritage.heritage.edu/ICS/Academics/SOWK/SOWK_531/2526_SP-SOWK_531-0/💻_W-06_223-31.jnz?portlet=Group_Discussion_Forums&screen=PostView&screenType=change&id=d68a189d-ff7f-49d7-835c-6b0ed625e6f6).
- Drawing on their practicum placements, students share real-world data collection practices and agency reporting processes in [Examples of Data Collection in Practicum Settings](https://myheritage.heritage.edu/ICS/Academics/SOWK/SOWK_531/2526_SP-SOWK_531-0/💻_W-06_223-31.jnz?portlet=Group_Discussion_Forums&screen=PostView&screenType=change&id=24a60a3d-3200-43d0-89d0-18dd6dc762d3).
- [Published Program Evaluations](https://myheritage.heritage.edu/ICS/Academics/SOWK/SOWK_531/2526_SP-SOWK_531-0/💻_W-06_223-31.jnz?portlet=Group_Discussion_Forums&screen=PostView&screenType=change&id=93240cd2-ab98-4f54-9a03-38964f64e6fe) asks students to find and share examples of published program evaluations.
