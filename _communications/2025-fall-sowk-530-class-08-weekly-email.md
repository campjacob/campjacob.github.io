## Unit Introduction and What You Will Learn

Week eight is a synchronous week, with an in-person session taking place on Saturday (10/18/25). The reading is focused on how to engage and intervene with families. There are forums asking students to consider home visits, the engagement process, and some of the strategies used with families discussed in Hepworth et al. (2023). During the class session, we are engaging in an all-class period activity we are calling the _Simulated Home Visit Experience_. We will partner with advanced-standing students who will act as simulated clients. Students will then have the opportunity to progress through three different stations, each with unique family needs and activities to engage in.

Some of the learning objectives for this week include:

- Describe the steps and strategies for engaging families during initial sessions across diverse practice settings.  
- Evaluate the benefits, challenges, and safety considerations of conducting home visits versus office-based sessions.  
- Engage effectively with clients and families in home-based settings, demonstrating empathy, warmth, and professionalism.
- Apply active listening, reflection, and summarizing skills to promote trust and rapport.
