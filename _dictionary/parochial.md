# parochial


## Adjective

1. relating to or supported by or located in a parish; “parochial schools”
	- Pertains to
		- parish
	- Related
		- parish
		- parish
2. narrowly restricted in outlook or scope; “little sympathy with parochial mentality”; “insular attitudes toward foreigners”
	- Synonyms
		- insular
	- Similar to
		- provincial
