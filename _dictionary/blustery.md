# blustery


## Adjective

1. blowing in violent and abrupt bursts; “blustering (or blusterous) winds of Patagonia”; “a cold blustery day”; “a gusty storm with strong sudden rushes of wind”
	- Synonyms
		- blustering
		- blusterous
	- Similar to
		- stormy
	- Related
		- bluster
		- bluster
2. noisily domineering; tending to browbeat others
	- Synonyms
		- bullying
	- Similar to
		- domineering
