# zygote


## Noun

1. (genetics) the diploid cell resulting from the union of a haploid spermatozoon and ovum (including the organism that develops from that cell)
	- Synonyms
		- fertilized ovum
	- Less specific
		- cell
	- More specific
		- heterozygote
		- homozygote
