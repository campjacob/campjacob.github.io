# chattel


## Noun

1. personal as opposed to real property; any tangible movable property (furniture or domestic animals or a car etc)
	- Synonyms
		- personal chattel
		- movable
	- Less specific
		- personal property
		- personal estate
		- personalty
		- private property
	- Topic Members
		- furniture
		- piece of furniture
		- article of furniture
		- car
		- auto
		- automobile
		- machine
		- motorcar
