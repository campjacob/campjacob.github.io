# Interlocutor


## Noun

1. the performer in the middle of a minstrel line who engages the others in talk
	- Synonyms
		- middleman
	- Less specific
		- minstrel
	- Member of
		- minstrel show
2. a person who takes part in a conversation
	- Synonyms
		- conversational partner
	- Less specific
		- conversationalist
		- conversationist
		- schmoozer
