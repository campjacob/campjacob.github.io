# locum tenens


## Noun

1. someone (physician or clergyman) who substitutes temporarily for another member of the same profession
	- Synonyms
		- locum
	- Less specific
		- stand-in
		- substitute
		- relief
		- reliever
		- backup
		- backup man
		- fill-in
