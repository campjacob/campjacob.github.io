# pernicious


## Adjective

1. exceedingly harmful
	- Synonyms
		- baneful
		- deadly
		- pestilent
	- Similar to
		- noxious
	- Related
		- pestilence
		- canker
		- perniciousness
		- toxicity
		- deadliness
		- lethality
2. working or spreading in a hidden and usually injurious way; “glaucoma is an insidious disease”; “a subtle poison”
	- Synonyms
		- insidious
		- subtle
	- Similar to
		- harmful
	- Related
		- perniciousness
		- toxicity
		- insidiousness
