# vermiform


## Adjective

1. resembling a worm; long and thin and cylindrical
	- Synonyms
		- worm-shaped
	- Similar to
		- formed

# vermiform appendix


## Noun

1. a vestigial process that extends from the lower end of the cecum and that resembles a small pouch
	- Synonyms
		- appendix
		- vermiform process
		- cecal appendage
	- Less specific
		- process
		- outgrowth
		- appendage
	- Part of
		- cecum
		- caecum
		- blind gut
