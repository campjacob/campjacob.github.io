# panacea


## Noun

1. (Greek mythology) the goddess of healing; daughter of Aesculapius and sister of Hygeia
	- Is a
		- Greek deity
2. hypothetical remedy for all ills or diseases; once sought by the alchemists
	- Synonyms
		- nostrum
		- catholicon
		- cure-all
	- Less specific
		- remedy
		- curative
		- cure
		- therapeutic
	- More specific
		- elixir
