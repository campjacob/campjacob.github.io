# avant-garde


## Noun

1. any creative group active in the innovation and application of new concepts and techniques in a given field (especially in the arts)
	- Synonyms
		- vanguard
		- van
		- new wave
	- Less specific
		- artistic movement
		- art movement

## Adjective

1. radically new or original; “an avant-garde theater piece”
	- Synonyms
		- daring
	- Similar to
		- original
