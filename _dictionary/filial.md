# filial


## Adjective

1. designating the generation or the sequence of generations following the parental generation
	- Antonyms
		- parental
	- Pertains to
		- offspring
		- progeny
		- issue
	- Topic Members
		- genetics
		- genetic science
2. relating to or characteristic of or befitting an offspring; “filial respect”
	- Antonyms
		- parental
		- maternal
		- paternal
	- Similar to
		- daughterly
