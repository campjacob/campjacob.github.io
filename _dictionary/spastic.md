# spastic


## Noun

1. a person suffering from spastic paralysis
	- Less specific
		- paralytic

	- Related

		- spastic paralysis
		- spasticity
2. suffering from spastic paralysis; “a spastic child”
	- Similar to
		- ill
		- sick
	- Related
		- cerebral palsy
		- spastic paralysis
		- spasticity
