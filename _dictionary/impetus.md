# impetus


## Noun

1. a force that moves something along
	- Synonyms
		- drift
		- impulsion
	- Less specific
		- force
	- Related
		- impel
		- force
		- drift
		- float
		- drift
		- be adrift
		- blow
2. the act of applying force suddenly; “the impulse knocked him over”
	- Synonyms
		- impulse
		- impulsion
	- Less specific
		- drive
		- thrust
		- driving force
	- Related
		- propel
		- impel
