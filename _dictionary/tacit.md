# tacit


## Adjective

1. implied by or inferred from actions or statements; “gave silent consent”; “a tacit agreement”; “the understood provisos of a custody agreement”
	- Synonyms
		- silent
		- understood
	- Similar to
		- implicit
		- inexplicit
