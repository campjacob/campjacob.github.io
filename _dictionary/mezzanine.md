# mezzanine


## Noun

1. first or lowest balcony
	- Synonyms
		- first balcony
	- Less specific
		- balcony
	- Parts
		- loge
2. intermediate floor just above the ground floor
	- Synonyms
		- mezzanine floor
		- entresol
	- Less specific
		- floor
		- level
		- storey
		- story
