# anathema


## Noun

1. a detested person; “he is an anathema to me”
	- Synonyms
		- bete noire
	- Less specific
		- unpleasant person
		- disagreeable person
2. a formal ecclesiastical curse accompanied by excommunication
	- Less specific
		- execration
		- condemnation
		- curse
	- Related
		- accurse
		- execrate
		- anathemize
		- comminate
		- anathemise
		- anathematize
