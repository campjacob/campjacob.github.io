# prolific


## Adjective

1. intellectually productive; “a prolific writer”; “a fecund imagination”
	- Synonyms
		- fecund
		- fertile
	- Similar to
		- productive
	- Related
		- fecundity
		- fruitfulness
2. bearing in abundance especially offspring; “flying foxes are extremely prolific”; “a prolific pear tree”
	- Synonyms
		- fertile
	- Similar to
		- fruitful
	- Related
		- fertility
		- fecundity
