# Kafkaesque


## Adjective

1. relating to or in the manner of Franz Kafka or his writings
	- Pertains to
		- Kafka
		- Franz Kafka
2. characterized by surreal distortion and a sense of impending danger; “the kafkaesque terror of the endless interrogations”
	- Similar to
		- unrealistic
