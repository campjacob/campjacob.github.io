# parallax


## Noun

1. the apparent displacement of an object as seen from two different points that are not on a line with the object
	- Less specific
		- optical phenomenon
	- More specific
		- heliocentric parallax
		- annual parallax
		- geocentric parallax
		- diurnal parallax
